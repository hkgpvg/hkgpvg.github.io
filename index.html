About Hanyi

Hanyi is a poet whose words capture the essence of human experience with grace and authenticity. Her poetry explores themes of memory, displacement, family, and the quiet moments that shape our lives.

Through her verses, Hanyi invites readers to pause, reflect, and find beauty in the everyday— from the steam rising from a grandmother's cookbook to the silence of a city burning. Each poem is a journey, a window into worlds both intimate and universal.
