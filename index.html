About Hanyi

Hanyi Zhou is a young poet from Hong Kong. Her work has appeared or is forthcoming in The Minnesota Review, Zone 3, and Sonora Review. Her poems have been recognized by Bennington Young Writers, Scholastic Art & Writing, and the Nancy Thorp Poetry Contest, among others.

She is always collecting small stories from the people she meets and finds joy in all forms of literature. She enjoys cooking and would love to hear about your favorite family recipe.
