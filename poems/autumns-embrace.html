Autumn's Embrace

by Hanyi

Leaves of gold and amber,
dance upon the autumn breeze,
nature's final celebration,
before winter's quiet freeze.

The air grows crisp and cool,
as summer fades away,
and autumn wraps the world,
in colors bright as day.

Crimson maples, golden oaks,
paint the landscape far and wide,
each leaf a fleeting moment,
each branch a source of pride.

In autumn's gentle embrace,
we learn to let things go,
to find beauty in the falling,
and peace in what we know.
