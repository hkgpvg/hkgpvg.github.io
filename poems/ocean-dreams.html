Ocean Dreams

by Hanyi

Waves crash against the shore,
carrying secrets from afar,
each ripple tells a story,
of where we've been and are.

The ocean breathes in rhythm,
a heartbeat deep and true,
its waters hold the memories,
of skies both gray and blue.

Salt upon the morning breeze,
seagulls dancing overhead,
the endless horizon beckons,
where dreams and tides are fed.

In ocean dreams I wander,
through depths of time and space,
and find within these waters,
my soul's most sacred place.
