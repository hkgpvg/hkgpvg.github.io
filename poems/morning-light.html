Morning Light

by Hanyi

The sun creeps through the curtains,
painting shadows on the wall,
a gentle whisper of new beginnings,
calling softly to us all.

Golden threads of morning light,
weave through windows clear and bright,
each ray a promise, each beam a song,
of days ahead where we belong.

In this quiet, peaceful hour,
before the world begins to stir,
we find ourselves in morning's grace,
renewed, refreshed, in time and space.

So let the light pour in today,
let it guide you on your way,
for every dawn brings hope anew,
and morning light is made for you.
