the sweetest honey in town

by Hanyi

honey!
organic!
fresh from the comb!
the sweetest honey out there!
saccharine and packaged.
hive buzzing with open, blistering sores and
honey brimming from the sides of a
plastic teddy bear jar,
wrists turning and flicking syrup of
pests, not insects,
empty spiracles,
graceless carcasses of
stingless bees scattered on the floor,
black boots on bodies,
crackle-crack like
babies teething,
wailing ghosts calling for
a queen.
spine bones on gravel,
a mangrove forest straining in
the silent indigo night.
